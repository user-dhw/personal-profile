RESEARCH PROJECT

  • A Comparative Study of Confucian and Aristotle’s Virtue Theories: From Zhong Yong to the Golden Mean, 2023
    Summary: As people in the world became more and more extreme, many severe problems occurred. This reminds me of the theories of Zhong Yong of Confucianist philosophy and the Golden Mean in Aristotle’s virtue ethics. This paper discusses the characteristics, similarities, and differences of two virtue theories from different traditions, hoping to give insights on the problem of extremity of the modern world.
  • The Vitality of Esperanto: To be or not to be?, 2022
    Publication: Yutian, Li, “The Vitality of Esperanto: To be or not to be?”, 3rd International Conference on Language, Art and Cultural Exchange (ICLACE 2022), July 14, 2022. • DOI: 10.2991/assehr.k.220706.012 Website: https://www.atlantis-press.com/proceedings/iclace-22/125976131
  • The Status of Popular Slang in Modern Mandarin, 2022
    Summary: As modern Mandarin develops, popular slang words are becoming more and more common. However, it has always been an arguable topic in academia. This article discusses the status of popular slang words in modern Mandarin by investigating their social and cultural contexts, origins, development & use.
  • Field Researcher of Linguistic Abilities of the Elderly, 2023
    Researched about the oral and auditory abilities of elderly people by conducting interviews, following Prof. Gu Yueguo of Beijing Foreign Language University. (Program code of National Social Science Fund of China: 21&ZD294)
